Grand Canyon Rafting

Location: Grand Canyon, Arizona, USA

Duration: 6 Days, 5 Nights

The Grand Canyon offers some of the most challenging and rewarding rafting experiences. This trip takes you through dramatic landscapes and thrilling rapids. Along the way, you’ll stop for hiking, camping, and even a swim in the Colorado River.

Highlights:

  • Stunning canyon views
  • Whitewater rapids
  • Campsites along the river
  • Historic sites and natural wonders

Futaleufú River Rafting

Location: Futaleufú, Chile

Duration: 4 Days, 3 Nights

Known for its turquoise waters and high-adrenaline rapids, the Futaleufú River offers a world-class rafting experience. This trip is perfect for adventure seekers looking for an exciting challenge.

Highlights:

  • Wild and remote landscapes
  • Challenging Class V rapids
  • Visit local Chilean villages
  • Unparalleled natural beauty

Ganges River Rafting

Location: Rishikesh, India

Duration: 3 Days, 2 Nights

For those seeking both adventure and spiritual enrichment, rafting on the Ganges River offers a unique experience. Flowing through the foothills of the Himalayas, this river is known for its clean, clear waters and scenic surroundings.

>Highlights:

  • Rafting through scenic mountain ranges
  • Visit ancient temples along the river
  • Enjoy peaceful sunrise yoga sessions
  • Exciting rapids and calmer stretches
